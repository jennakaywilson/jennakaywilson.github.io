The further my work progresses the more it becomes about creating an experience for the viewer. I am striving to pull people in and change the environment for even a brief moment. My most recent work has been mixed media collages using a power sander to blend layers of acrylic paint and construction paper. However, I have also created a number of installations using a mixture of digital and analog mediums. I am drawn to color, movement, and light.

Following my graduation from Brigham Young University in December of 2024 I plan to pursue a full-time career in artmaking and art education. For me, teaching has always been about creating a safe environment for students to take risks and be creative.
